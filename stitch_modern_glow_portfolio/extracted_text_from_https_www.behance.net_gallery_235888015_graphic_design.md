# GRAPHIC DESIGN PORTFOLIO 2025 | ATIKUR RAHMAN

[Atikur Rahman](https://www.behance.net/atikrehman)•

Follow Following Unfollow

[](/gallery/235888015/GRAPHIC-DESIGN-PORTFOLIO-2025-ATIKUR-RAHMAN/modules/1355589539)

Hi, I’m Atikur Rahman – a passionate Graphic Designer & Mentor with 5+ years of experience in turning ideas into impactful visuals.

  


I am currently working as a Team Leader – Graphic Design Department at Orbit Technology, where I’ve been contributing for almost 5 years in a fully remote role. Leading a creative team has sharpened my ability to handle large-scale design projects, collaborate across departments, and deliver high-quality results consistently under tight deadlines.

  


I specialize in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Blender, creating everything from social media campaigns, advanced poster designs, and branding to 3D product ads and digital manipulation.

  


Beyond design work, I’ve trained 1,000+ students through my signature courses, including Graphic Design – Your Ultimate Savior and Photoshop Wizard. My goal is simple: to help aspiring designers unlock their creativity and reach professional-level skills.

  


I’m also building my YouTube channel, where I share beginner-to-pro tutorials, design principles, and creative workflows — making design education accessible, engaging, and practical for everyone.

  


At the core of my work, I believe design isn’t just about aesthetics; it’s about storytelling, problem-solving, and creating a lasting impact that connects with people on a deeper level.
